Whole Wheat Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Spaghetti
  • One tablespoon of Salt
  • Any Spaghetti Sauce will work for this

Instructions

  1. 3 Quarts of Water
  2. Bring Water to Boil and Add 1 Tablespoon of Salt
  3. When the Water start boiling, Add Whole Wheat Pasta
  4. Stir Occasionally and cook for 10 minutes

Serve and Enjoy
