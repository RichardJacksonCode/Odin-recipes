How to make Macaroni Elbows

Ingredients

  • A packet of Allegra's Macaroni Elbows
  • Salt

That it... really

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil 1 quart of water and Add salt as you desire
  2. Add Pasta and Stir Occasionally for 10 minutes
  3. Drain well and serve as desire

What else after cooking the pasta

Well, after cooking the pasta you can add whatever you want to do with it. you can add ground beef, milk, butter, or whatever you want.

Serve and Enjoy
