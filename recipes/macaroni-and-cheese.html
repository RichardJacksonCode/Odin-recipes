How to make Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup of Water
  • 1/4 Cup of Milk
  • 1/4 Cup of Butter or Margarine
  • 1 Packet of Premier Pantry's Macaroni and Cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil 6 Cup of Water and Add Salt at taste
  2. Stir Pasta and boil for 10 minutes
  3. Drain the Water and return pasta to pot.
  4. Add 1/4 Cup of Margarine or butter
  5. Add 1/4 Cup of Milk and Content of cheese sauce packet
  6. Mix well until the pasta is smooth and creamy

Serve and Enjoy
